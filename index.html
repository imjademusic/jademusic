News

‘Elements’

[06/02/21]

Hi everyone! My new Ep ‘Elements’ it’s out now on Spotify. It’s my first release and first personal album. "Elements" is the culmination of months of work where I have expressed my feelings in each of the songs. I am very proud of this album, but it will not be the last. This album revolves around the five elements of the Chinese culture: Water, Earth, Wood, Fire and Metal. Each element has its own meaning and feeling. Hope you like it and enjoy it!

‘Don't forget to smell the flowers’

[12/03/21]

Jade’s new single is out now on Spotify, Youtube and more music platforms. This new release is a sequel of the last EP ‘Elements’. With more electronic sounds, synths but also adding a drum & bass background. This is the first single of my new album not named yet. I want to create new sounds and new music for all people who want to chill and experiment sensations and emotions. Don’t forget to smell the flowers tries to combine genres like downtempo, ambient, chillout, electronic and drum & bass. It’s the first time I create a single with lots of different types of genres. An experimental song I hope you enjoy and like!

About

Born in Barcelona (Spain), Marc Sánchez - more commonly known as Jade - is a producer of electronic, ambient and chill out music. He just started a musical career with his first album - Elements- in February 2021. Self-taught in music, starts early playing electric guitar and piano. Afterward, he starts playing synth with some bands. Years later, and thanks to the inspiration of other musicians, he began to produce on his own. A unique and different sound.
